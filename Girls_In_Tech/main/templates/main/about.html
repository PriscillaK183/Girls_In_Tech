About Us

Girls In Tech is a platform dedicated to inspiring young women to explore, learn, and create using technology.

We believe in building confidence through creativity and code.

Our Mission

To empower girls with the tools, mentorship, and community they need to thrive in the tech world.

What We Offer

  • Free coding workshops and tutorials
  • Mentorship from women in tech industries
  • Community forums to share ideas and support
  • Creative challenges and hackathons

Why It Matters

Technology shapes the future — and every girl deserves a voice in that future. We’re here to make sure she has one.

Join Us

Whether you're just curious or already coding, Girls In Tech welcomes you. Sign up, share your story, and grow with us!
